Quality Management

Quality Policy

We will deliver quality as defined by the customer, at a profit if we can, and at a loss if we must.

Quality System

  • TQM approach adopted
  • Quality is taken ona company wide level
  • Quality System in BENL is christened "Excellence through change"(EFC)
  • Quality teams have been set up; quality coordinator and "change agent" ie facilitators have been appointed.
  • Processes are being defined/developed and documented.

Quality Assurance and Control

  • Commitment to quality enshrined in quality policy manual
  • Coordination from the office of the Director of operation
  • Quality assurance program (QAP) established to achieve desired quality
  • QAP applies to all areas of design, engineering, fabrication and quality control
  • Regular meetings of top management to renew the QAP.
